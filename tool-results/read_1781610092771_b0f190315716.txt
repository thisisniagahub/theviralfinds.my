   304→## 6. Profit Optimization — ROI Calculation for Affiliate Marketing
   305→
   306→### Key Data Points for ROI
   307→
   308→| Metric | What It Means | Typical Range | Formula |
   309→|--------|--------------|---------------|---------|
   310→| **Commission Rate** | % of sale you earn | 2.5%–40% (Shopee: 2.5%–10% base + up to 40% brand Xtra) | Set by merchant |
   311→| **Conversion Rate** | % of clicks that result in sales | 0.5%–1% average, 3%+ excellent | (Conversions ÷ Clicks) × 100 |
   312→| **Click-Through Rate (CTR)** | % of impressions that get clicks | 0.5%–1% average, >1% excellent | (Clicks ÷ Impressions) × 100 |
   313→| **Earnings Per Click (EPC)** | Revenue per click | Varies widely | Total Commissions ÷ Total Clicks |
   314→| **Average Order Value (AOV)** | Average purchase amount | Product-dependent | Total Revenue ÷ Number of Orders |
   315→| **Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)** | Total value per customer | Relevant for subscription products | Avg Purchase Value × Purchase Frequency × Customer Lifespan |
   316→
   317→### Profit Calculation Formulas
   318→
   319→**Simple ROI Formula:**
   320→```
   321→ROI = ((Revenue - Cost) / Cost) × 100%
   322→
   323→Example:
   324→- Commission rate: 8%
   325→- Product price: RM100
   326→- Commission per sale: RM8
   327→- Ad spend per click: RM0.50
   328→- Conversion rate: 2%
   329→- Clicks needed per sale: 50 (1/0.02)
   330→- Cost per sale: 50 × RM0.50 = RM25
   331→- ROI = ((RM8 - RM25) / RM25) × 100% = -68% ← NOT PROFITABLE
   332→
   333→For profitability:
   334→- Need commission > cost per sale
   335→- RM8 commission means you need cost per sale < RM8
   336→- At RM0.50/click: need conversion rate > 6.25% OR find products with higher commission
   337→```
   338→
   339→**Profitability Score Algorithm:**
   340→```
   341→Profit Score = (Commission Rate × AOV × Conversion Rate) / (Competition Level × Content Cost)
   342→
   343→Higher score = more profitable product to promote
   344→
   345→Weighted factors:
   346→- Commission Rate: 25% weight
   347→- Conversion Rate: 30% weight  
   348→- AOV: 20% weight
   349→- Product Rating: 10% weight
   350→- Sales Volume: 10% weight
   351→- Competition Level: 5% weight (inverse)
   352→```
   353→
   354→### Industry Benchmarks (Affiliate Marketing)
   355→- **Average ROI**: 12:1 ($12 earned for every $1 spent on affiliate programs)
   356→- **Conversion rate**: 1–3% across industries; exceptional programs hit 5%+
   357→- **Commission rates**: 5–30% typical; Shopee offers 2.5%–10% base + up to 40% brand bonus
   358→- **CTR**: 0.5%–1% average; above 1% is excellent

   434→def calculate_product_score(product):
   435→    """Score products for affiliate profitability"""
   436→    score = 0
   437→    
   438→    # Commission Score (25%)
   439→    commission = product.commission_rate
   440→    if commission >= 10: score += 25
   441→    elif commission >= 5: score += 18
   442→    elif commission >= 3: score += 10
   443→    else: score += 5
   444→    
   445→    # Conversion Score (30%) — estimated from category averages + product signals
   446→    est_conversion = estimate_conversion_rate(product)
   447→    if est_conversion >= 0.03: score += 30
   448→    elif est_conversion >= 0.02: score += 22
   449→    elif est_conversion >= 0.01: score += 15
   450→    else: score += 5
   451→    
   452→    # Rating Score (10%)
   453→    if product.rating >= 4.8: score += 10
   454→    elif product.rating >= 4.5: score += 7
   455→    elif product.rating >= 4.0: score += 4
   456→    else: score += 0
   457→    
   458→    # Sales Volume Score (10%)
   459→    if product.monthly_sold >= 10000: score += 10
   460→    elif product.monthly_sold >= 1000: score += 7
   461→    elif product.monthly_sold >= 100: score += 4
   462→    else: score += 2
   463→    
   464→    # Price Sweet Spot (10%) — RM20-100 best for impulse
   465→    if 20 <= product.price <= 100: score += 10
   466→    elif 10 <= product.price <= 200: score += 6
   467→    else: score += 2
   468→    
   469→    # Competition Score (10%) — inverse
   470→    competition = get_competition_level(product)
   471→    if competition == 'low': score += 10
   472→    elif competition == 'medium': score += 6
   473→    else: score += 2
   474→    
   475→    # Trend Momentum (5%)
   476→    trend = get_trend_momentum(product.name)
   477→    if trend == 'rising': score += 5
   478→    elif trend == 'stable': score += 3
   479→    else: score += 0
   480→    
   481→    return score
   482→
   483→# Profit estimate per 1000 clicks
   484→def estimate_profit(product, clicks=1000):
   485→    commission_per_sale = product.price * (product.commission_rate / 100)
   486→    est_conversions = clicks * estimate_conversion_rate(product)
   487→    est_revenue = est_conversions * commission_per_sale
   488→    return est_revenue
   489→```
